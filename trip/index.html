Beginner Tips

Take a Lesson

Day 1: Book a 2-3h group lesson. Essential for safety and faster learning. 26-40 EUR/person.

Rest Days Matter

Plan at least 1 non-skiing activity. Prevents injury from muscle fatigue and keeps it fun.

Rent at Resort

Beginner skis are shorter/forgiving. Staff can adjust bindings. Don't bring your own first time.

Prep Your Body

Start stretching legs/core 1 week before. Expect sore muscles days 2-3. Stay hydrated!
